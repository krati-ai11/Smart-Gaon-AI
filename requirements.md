# Smart Gaon AI - Requirements

## Problem Statement
Many villages lack access to proper healthcare, market price updates, and government schemes information.

## Proposed Solution
Smart Gaon AI is an AI-based platform that helps rural citizens get instant information about:
- Government schemes
- Crop prices
- Basic healthcare guidance
- Local job opportunities

## Target Users
- Farmers
- Rural students
- Small shop owners
- Village households

## Key Features
- AI Chat Assistant in Hindi
- Voice support for illiterate users
- Offline mode for low internet areas
- Simple mobile interface

## Expected Impact
- Better awareness
- Improved decision making
- Digital empowerment of rural India
